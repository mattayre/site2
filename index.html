Risk Assessment Services

There are specific legislative and best practice requirements your company must meet and here at Peace of Mind we offer you a comprehensive service that ensures whatever your risk assessment requirements are, we can help

Comprehensive workplace assessment

If you think your company risks not complying with current regulations in various areas of your business, we offer a comprehensive risk assessment survey of your workplace, with a compreshensive report and follow up advice where applicable

Fire risk assessment

Our Fire Risk Assessment service has been designed specifically to help you comply, whilst also prioritising areas for future attention. A consultant will assess a broad range of risk factors including sources of ignition, means of escape, warning systems, risk to personnel and many more.

Noise risk assessment

Excessive noise at work is a significant yet preventable problem affecting thousands of UK workplaces. With our consultancy service a fully competent noise assessor (recognised by the Institute of Acoustics), will visit your premises to determine noise exposures using appropriate instrumentation, calibration, measurement and analysis techniques.

DSE risk assessment

There are specific legislative and best practice requirements for the use of display screen equipment(DSE) such as computers and their configuration and use. Our consultants can assist organisations to comply with these requirements and thereby protect their staff from adverse health effects.

Health and Safety Policy

Health and safety policies form the foundation of managing safety, so getting them right is absolutely fundamental. Your health and safety policy is unique to your organisation and must be legally compliant. Furthermore, in order to keep pace with changes, health and safety policies must be regularly reviewed. If you think your health and safety policy could benefit then consider our health and safety policy review service
